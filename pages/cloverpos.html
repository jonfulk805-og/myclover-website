AI-Powered

Chappie Runs Your Inventory So You Don't Have To

Every CloverPOS installation has a private AI agent powered by Ollama. It learns your business, watches your stock, and tells you what to do before you know to ask.

🧠 Demand Forecasting

Chappie analyzes sales patterns by day, week, season. Knows you sell more sunscreen in June and more hot chocolate in December. Adjusts reorder suggestions automatically.

🚨 Smart Reorder Alerts

"Hey — you're down to 12 units of SKU #4421 and you normally sell 8/week. Reorder now or you'll be out by Thursday." That's Chappie talking.

💣 Dead Stock Detection

"These 47 items haven't moved in 90 days. Want me to suggest a clearance price?" Chappie finds dead weight before it becomes a write-off.

💬 Natural Language Queries

Don't search menus. Just ask: "What's my best-selling item this month?" or "Show me everything from supplier XYZ." Chappie answers instantly.

🏷️ Auto-Categorization

Add a new product, Chappie suggests the category, tax class, and similar items already in stock. Bulk import a CSV? It categorizes everything.

📊 Sales Intelligence

"Tuesdays are your slowest day. Run a promo?" Chappie spots patterns humans miss and suggests actions that make money.
