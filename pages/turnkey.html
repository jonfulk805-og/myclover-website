❓ FAQ

Common Questions

Is this a franchise? ▼
No. This is a software license, not a franchise. You're purchasing a business template and platform access. There's no franchise fee, no territory restrictions (unless you purchase exclusivity), and no FTC Franchise Rule compliance needed. You operate independently under your own brand.
Do I keep 100% of my customer revenue? ▼
Yes. Revenue from your customers goes directly to your Stripe account. MCT earns only through the monthly platform fee, CloverCoin ecosystem participation, and marketplace commissions — never from your direct sales.
Do I need technical experience? ▼
Minimal. Each business comes with a step-by-step 30-day launch playbook. The website is pre-built, payments are pre-configured, and Viktor AI can help you customize. For hardware-based businesses (drones, mesh radio), the supplier guides walk you through sourcing and assembly.
Can I use the "Clover" brand name? ▼
No — mandatory rebrand is required. Your business runs under your own brand name and identity. "Powered by MyClover.Tech" may appear in the footer only. This protects both parties and keeps the ecosystem clean.
Can I resell my business later? ▼
Yes. You can transfer or sell your operating business to another party. There's a $99 transfer fee to update the platform license. MCT takes no cut of the resale price — you keep the full sale amount.
What if I stop paying the monthly platform fee? ▼
Your website and core business tools continue to work — you own the code. Platform features like CloverCoin integration, marketplace listing, AI assistance, and priority support would be disabled. No data is deleted.
Do you accept crypto? ▼
Yes. License purchases can be made via Stripe (credit card), CloverCoin, or XRPL-based cryptocurrency. Contact inforequest@myclover.tech for crypto payment arrangements.
Can I customize beyond the template? ▼
Absolutely. The template is your starting point, not your ceiling. You own the code and can modify anything. Viktor AI can help with customizations, and the creator community in Slack can provide guidance and collaboration.
