Under the Hood

Open-Source Foundation. Premium Execution.

Built on battle-tested open-source VR software, wrapped in the MyClover.Tech experience. We credit every creator and contribute back.

🤝

Monado (OpenXR)

Open-source OpenXR runtime by Collabora. The industry-standard VR API. BSL-1.0

📡

ALVR

Air Light VR — wireless VR streaming from PC to standalone headsets. Ultra-low latency. MIT

🌐

Overte

Open-source social VR platform, continuing High Fidelity's vision. Custom worlds, spatial audio. Apache-2.0

🎮

Godot 4 VR

Open-source game engine with built-in VR toolkit. Scene building, 3D content creation. MIT

💻

Blender

3D modeling, rendering, and animation. Headless render pipeline for VR asset generation. GPL-2.0+

🤖

Ollama AI

Local LLMs powering Chappie & Clover in VR. Voice commands, NPC AI, scene generation. MIT
