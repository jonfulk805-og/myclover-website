AI Design & Engineering

Meet CloverDesign

Two AI agents — Designer Bot and Engineer Bot — turn your raw ideas into professional plans with specs, drawings, and blueprints. Home layouts, engineering designs, architectural visualization — all self-hosted on your CloverStack.

💰 See Pricing Meet the Bots → Features
AI Agents

Two Bots. One Pipeline. Idea to Blueprint.

Describe what you want in plain English. Designer Bot visualizes it. Engineer Bot specs it.

🎨 Designer Bot

The creative brain. Takes raw ideas and turns them into visuals you can see, refine, and approve.

  • Floor plans & room layouts from text descriptions
  • Interior design — furniture, finishes, lighting, color palettes
  • Exterior visualization — facades, landscaping, curb appeal
  • 3D concept renders with AI image generation
  • Mood boards & material palettes auto-generated from style prompts
  • Renovation planning — before/after visualization
  • Product & brand mockups
  • Iterative refinement — describe changes, Designer Bot revises

📐 Engineer Bot

The technical brain. Converts approved designs into buildable, spec'd, code-compliant plans.

  • Technical drawings — blueprints with dimensions, scale, annotations
  • Structural specs — load calcs, material strengths, foundation requirements
  • Bill of Materials (BOM) — itemized parts, quantities, cost estimates
  • Code compliance — IRC/IBC residential, ADA, local building code flags
  • MEP layouts — mechanical, electrical, plumbing rough-in diagrams
  • Cost estimation — labor + materials breakdown by trade
  • Export: PDF blueprints, DXF/DWG (CAD), SVG, 3D models (STL/OBJ)

Capabilities

Everything You Need to Design and Plan

Professional-grade design tools, powered by AI, running on your hardware.

🏠

Home Design & Layout

Complete home design — floor plans, room layouts, furniture placement, traffic flow analysis. From single rooms to entire homes. Interactive drag-and-drop with AI suggestions.

🖼️

AI Renders & Visualization

Photorealistic renders generated locally with Stable Diffusion XL via ComfyUI. See your design before building. Interior, exterior, aerial views, day/night lighting.

📋

Blueprint Generation

Proper technical drawings with dimensions, scale bars, section cuts, and annotations. PDF export in standard architectural scales (1/4" = 1', 1:50, etc.).

🧮

BOM & Cost Estimation

Automatic bill of materials with quantities, unit costs, and labor estimates. Breakdown by trade — framing, electrical, plumbing, finishes. Regional price adjustments.

📏

Parametric 3D Modeling

FreeCAD integration for precision 3D models. Change dimensions and everything updates. Export STL for 3D printing, OBJ for visualization, STEP for manufacturing.

⚡

MEP Planning

Mechanical, electrical, and plumbing rough-in diagrams. Circuit load calculations, pipe sizing, HVAC duct routing. Clash detection between systems.

✅

Code Compliance

AI-powered building code checks — IRC/IBC residential, ADA accessibility, egress requirements, structural minimums. Flags issues before you submit to the building dept.

💬

Natural Language Design

Powered by Chappie AI (Ollama). Describe what you want conversationally: "Add a bathroom off the master bedroom with a walk-in shower." The AI builds it.
